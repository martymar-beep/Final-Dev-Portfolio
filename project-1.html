Project 1

Responsive Product Splash Page

This project was a Scrimba assignment to create a mobile-first product splash page, which I personalized by inventing SocializeMe, a high-energy meetup group for weekend fun. I customized the layout, colors, and imagery to create a bold, upbeat brand. Along the way, I practiced building responsive layouts with Flexbox, structured content with modifier classes, and styled an accessible sign-up form to match the design.

What I Learned

This project gave me hands-on experience thinking in terms of mobile-first design and responsive behavior. I deepened my understanding of relative units, media queries, and content flow using Flexbox. I also gained confidence in aligning form styling with brand aesthetics and improving user experience across various devices.
